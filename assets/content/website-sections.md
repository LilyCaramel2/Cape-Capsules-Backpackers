## FAQ SECTION (Optional but Recommended)

### Section Title
**"Frequently Asked Questions"**

### Q1: What time is check-in and check-out?
**A:** Check-in is from 2:00 PM. Check-out is strictly at 10:00 AM to allow cleaning for incoming guests. Early check-in or late check-out may be available‚Äîask at reception.

### Q2: Is breakfast included?
**A:** Breakfast is available for R110 per person (toast, eggs, fruit, coffee/tea). Not included in standard rates but included in some packages.

### Q3: How does the deposit work?
**A:** All bookings require a R500 refundable deposit. Foreign guests pay cash (no Swift refunds); SA guests can pay by card with EFT refund. A cleaning fee is deducted.

### Q4: Can I smoke cannabis on the property?
**A:** Yes, in designated outdoor areas only (garden and balcony). No smoking inside the building. Must be 18+ and comply with South African cannabis laws.

### Q5: Is there WiFi?
**A:** Yes, fast and reliable WiFi throughout the property. We also have solar power, so no loadshedding interruptions.

### Q6: Can I rent surf equipment?
**A:** Yes! We rent wetsuits and surfboards. Please book at least one day in advance at reception.

### Q7: How far is the beach?
**A:** Literally across the road! East Beach and Surfers Corner are a 2-5 minute walk.

### Q8: Is parking available?
**A:** Street parking is available. Cape Town has standard urban parking considerations‚Äîask staff for tips.
